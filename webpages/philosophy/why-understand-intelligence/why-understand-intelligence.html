Why Understand Intelligence?

Thesis

The quest to understand intelligence is the most significant enterprise of human existence.

Mission

Define the principles of intelligence and apply that knowledge to build cognitive-capable technology.

Purpose

Ensure the survival, prosperity, and legacy of humankind.

Survival

The first purpose of AI research is the survival of mankind.

By applying our intelligence strengthened by a powerful mammalian neocortex, our species survives and thrives in a world where a significant population of mankind has transcended a naturally vicious life of unnecessary suffering and propensity for animalistic violence. This does not overlook or excuse the unique malevolence spawned from human cognitive abilities or the troubles people face today or throughout history. In fact, constant vigilance of these concepts are crucially important for the survival of our species. However despite this dark side, the ultimate source of human success and prosperity thus far is due to our intelligence.
