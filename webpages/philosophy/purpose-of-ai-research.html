Good [1] The quest to understand and apply the principles of intelligence is the most profound human philosophical, technical, and engineering endeavor. In fact, it may be the ultimate human invention, a wellspring of unimaginable benefits. Not only will understanding intelligence provide us remarkable technologies, but also it will provide a deeper understanding of the essence of being. Human existance is characterized by a fair bit of suffering. We're limited creatures and life is very hard. Everyone dies, everyone you love is going to die. All of the things you do will eventually fail. Suffering is a certainty.
