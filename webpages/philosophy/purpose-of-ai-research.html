Purpose of AI Research

Mankind's quest to understand and apply the principles of intelligence is the most significant endeavor of our existence. This is because the brain, the seat of our intelligence, is the most important six inches of our lives and the fundamental source of the blessings of modernity. Since the dawn of our earliest ancestors, human cognitive labor has reaped a bountiful intellectual, technological, and cultural harvest. This crop of the mind includes an inexhaustible list marvels, all the great wonders and comforts of the world that have made us thrive: reason, science, engineering, philosophy, agriculture, the free market, democracy, art, music, literature, poetry, drama, fiction, and many others. By using our intelligence, a significant population of mankind has transcended an otherwise vicious life of unnecessary suffering and a propensity for animalistic violence. This is not an attempt to overlook or excuse the cold reality of our ever-present animal nature, the unique malevolence spawned from human cognitive abilities, or the troubles people still face today. In fact, constant knowledge and reminder of these concepts are crucially important and will be discussed on this website. Despite our dark side, the ultimate source of human success and prosperity thus far is due to our intelligence.

Because intelligence is beneficial to all areas of our lives, the next logical step is to build intelligent machines. exponential tech, moores law Technological intelligence is the ultimate human invention because is and a wellspring of unimaginable benefits. Not only will understanding intelligence provide us remarkable technologies, but also it will provide a deeper understanding of the essence of being.
