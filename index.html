Neuroowl

Purpose

Neuroowl is a repository of evolving notes on various topics relevant to Artificial Intelligence(AI). It serves as an educational and referential resource for myself and other AI researchers. The field is a broad and has decades of progress in philosophy, neuroscience, and technology. Therefore, the topics presented are not exhaustive and chosen based on personal interests. Ultimately, the mission of this website is to serve humanity through understanding biological intelligence and developing machine intelligence.
