Neuroowl

Neuroowl is a repository of notes related to Neuroscience and Artificial Intelligence(AI). Ultimately, understanding the principles of intelligence and building technology with cognitive abilities will have profound impacts to humanity. This website attempts to understand the subjects surrounding intelligence and share the knowledge using precise language and clear visuals. The topics discussed are certainly not exhaustive and the ideas will change due to fresh perspectives, clarifications, and corrections. This website is currently under construction so please pardon any incomplete topics.
