What is Neuroowl?

Neuroowl is a repository of evolving notes on topics related to Biological and Artificial Intelligence(AI). The topics discussed are certainly not exhaustive and the writings will change due to new ideas, fresh perspectives, clarifications, and corrections.

Why Neuroowl?

Ultimately, understanding intelligence and building technology with cognitive abilities will have profound impacts to humanity. This website attempts to understand all subjects surrounding the complex principles of intelligence and share this knowledge with precise language and clear visuals.
